Spiral

For this project, I wanted to push myself and use a program I had never done before. I used Adobe After Effects to create an animation that flows with the music playing. I wanted to mimic the old screensaver animations that would play on computers during the early 2000s with crazy visuals and effects. I titled the piece "Spiral" due to the main tool function I used in After Effect called Spiral, as well as how our lives have begun to spiral out of control due to the lock down and stay at home order. I want my project to elicit a feeling of calm and relaxation with smooth video transitions and animations.
