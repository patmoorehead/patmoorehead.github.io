Final Project: Torii Gates

This is my final project, where created large Japanese torii gates in Minecraft, then printed the model out.

This is the largest print of the two, and it took roughly four and a half hours. It was not scaled down at all.

This is the second print I did, which took roughly two and a half hours. It was scaled down by one quarter from the first.

Both prints finished and scaffolding taken off.

After adding some jesso to smooth the edges, painted and finished.
